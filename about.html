Our Story

A Trusted Name in Healthcare
Across Borders

Metro Starcare Medical Centre, under the Metro Medical Group, has been a trusted healthcare provider for over a decade. With branches in Sharjah, UAE and Kuwait, we deliver state-of-the-art medical services tailored to individual needs.

From cutting-edge technology to a patient-centric approach, our experienced team ensures world-class care. Our mission is to make quality healthcare accessible and affordable globally, enhancing lives with a personal touch.

We treat all enquiries in confidence. We will work with you to develop individualised care plans, including management of chronic diseases. If we cannot assist, we provide referrals and advice about the type of care you require.
